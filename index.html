Supergirl

Summary

Supergirl is a movie series about a girl born with powers that comes from another planet
called Krypton. The movie series is about a little girl named Kara that had arrived second
from escaping a deadly explosion. Long story short, after arriving to Earth, Kara keeps her
powers a secret until 12 years later she was forced to use her powers in order to save her
adoptive sister from a plane crash. That was when she decided she wanted to become a
hero to save more people. Kara fights many battles throughout the season, the way her perseverance
and sense of justice pulls her through makes viewers like this show even more.

Two Main Characters

There are many amazing characters in Supergirl, but my favorites are; Kara Zor-El and Alex Danvers. Both characters play a big role in the show and are in most of the scenes that have to do with combat. Although there are other great characters in this movie like Matian Manhunter, I would have to say that Kara and Alex are my favorites.
