Alex Danvers

Actor Chyler Leigh plays as the character, Alex Danvers. Alex Danvers
is known for being Supergirl's older adoptive sister, as Alex's family took
Supergirl in after she had just landed on Earth. Alex is very caring of her
sister and she is also the bio-engineer + the director of the D.E.O., Which
stands for, Department of Extra-Normal Operations. Alex's backstory was
a rather unnormal one, since she had Kara by her side through every memory.
Kara and Alex share a strong bond with each other, and they both can't imagine
what it would be like without each other.
