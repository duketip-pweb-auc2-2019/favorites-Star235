Kara Zor-El

Actor Melissa Benoist, plays as Kara Zor-El, also known as Supergirl. Kara Zor-El is the main character
in the famous television series called, "Supergirl." Kara posseses special inhuman abilities that gives her
unlimited superhuman strength. Kara's backstory was a tragic one, though she enjoys living on earth, even
if she feels out of place. Kara was sent to earth after her planet was about to die completely, her main
mission was to take care of her little cousin, also known as Superman. But her pod got knocked off course
and suspended her in time, making her sleep for twenty-four years until the pod finally escaped. Once she
got to earth, her "little" cousin was now older than her.
